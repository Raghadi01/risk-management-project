# Risk Management Project

## Project Overview
Conducted a comprehensive security risk management assessment for a
retail organization with multiple branches. The assessment covered
asset identification, threat analysis, vulnerability mapping, risk
calculation, and control planning.

## What We Did

### 1. Asset Identification and Prioritization
- Identified and cataloged 16 organizational assets across 4 categories:
  Hardware (computers, routers, POS, security cameras, printers)
  Software (cloud server, firewall)
  Data (customer info, employee info, administration data)
  People (customers, managers, employees)
- Prioritized assets using a weighted scoring model across 4 criteria:
  revenue generation, replacement cost, public image impact,
  and criticality to operations

### 2. Threat Identification and Modeling
- Identified 15 cyber threats based on top 2024 cybersecurity threats
- Top threats: Data Breaches (92%), IoT Vulnerabilities (84%),
  Cloud Security Risks (84%)
- Built a Threat-Vulnerability-Asset (TVA) matrix mapping all
  threats to assets and vulnerabilities

### 3. Risk Calculation
- Calculated 71 individual risks using:
  Risk Rating = Asset Impact x Vulnerability Likelihood
- Classified risks into 3 levels:
  High (70+), Medium (40-69), Low (39 and below)

### 4. Risk Control Strategy
Applied 4 risk strategies based on risk level and control cost:
- Mitigate — reduce likelihood or impact
- Avoid — eliminate the risk entirely
- Transfer — shift risk to a third party
- Accept — acknowledge and monitor low risks

### 5. Incident Response Plan
Developed before, during, and after response procedures for:
- Ransomware attacks on endpoints
- Phishing attacks on cloud infrastructure
- Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs)
- Insider threat incidents

### 6. Disaster Recovery Plan
Created recovery procedures for high-priority risks including
supply chain attacks, zero-day exploits, and AI-powered threats

### 7. Business Continuity Plan
Defined continuity procedures to ensure operations continue
during and after security incidents

## Key Skills Applied
- Asset identification and weighted prioritization
- Threat modeling and TVA matrix development
- Quantitative risk calculation
- Risk control strategy selection
- Incident response planning
- Disaster recovery planning
- Business continuity planning
